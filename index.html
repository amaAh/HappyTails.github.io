Community Oriented

I've lived in the Capitol Hill area for nearly 15 years and know all the best parks and trails in our collective neighborhoods. HappyTrails is also a Capitol Hill Community member. In short CHC members loosely adhere to some pragmatic, self sustaining business practices that work as partial antithesis to the gentrification occurring on the hill.
